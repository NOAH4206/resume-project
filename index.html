About Me:

My name is Matthew Gross.
I was born in Connecticut, but moved to South Florida when I entered Kindergarten and have lived here ever since. I graduated from Broward College with a teaching degree in 2019. This is my fifth year teaching Engineering at Taravella High School, and I am excited to welcome you all to our wonderful community.
Outside of teaching, I am a giant nerd and love to play video games, Dungeons & Dragons, and board games. I also enjoy woodworking, computer programming, and painting in watercolors.
